Artisan Ceramic Vase

$65.00

Discover the understated elegance of our Artisan Ceramic Vase. Hand-thrown by skilled ceramists, this piece embodies minimalist design with a rich, tactile finish. Its unique texture and warm, natural tone make it a perfect standalone decor item or a beautiful vessel for your favorite blooms.

Each EleganceCraft vase is a testament to artisanal skill, crafted from high-quality stoneware and finished with a subtle glaze. Its organic silhouette and earthy palette are designed to complement a wide range of interior styles, from modern minimalist to rustic chic. Bring a piece of handcrafted artistry into your home.

Why you'll love it:

  • Elevates any space with its refined, minimalist aesthetic.
  • Serves as a unique focal point or a versatile accent piece.
  • Durable and timeless, designed to be cherished for years.
  • Supports traditional craftsmanship and independent artisans.

Product Specifications:

  • Material: Premium Stoneware Ceramic
  • Finish: Matte Glaze with natural texture
  • Color: Earthy Cream
  • Dimensions: 8" (H) x 6" (Dia) - (Approximate, due to handmade nature)
  • Care: Hand wash recommended.

How do I care for my ceramic vase?

We recommend hand washing your vase with mild soap and warm water. Avoid harsh abrasives to preserve its unique finish.

Is the vase waterproof?

Yes, all our ceramic vases are fully glazed internally to ensure they are watertight and suitable for fresh flowers.

What if my vase arrives damaged?

Please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or refund immediately.

We want you to be completely satisfied with your EleganceCraft purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a hassle-free return policy.

  • 30-Day Returns: You may return most new, unused items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
  • Original Condition: Items must be returned in their original packaging, unused, and in the same condition that you received them.
  • Return Shipping: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.
  • Processing Refunds: Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Custom-made items, perishable goods, and digital products are not eligible for return.
